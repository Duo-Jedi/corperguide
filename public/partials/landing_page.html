About {{appName}}

Corperzwee is a community that consists of both current and ex-corpers that have come together to help prospective corp members in making good decisions on their arrival at their state of deployment. These ranges from making choices about PPA, getting an apartment best situated closer to accepted PPA, places of worship and other landmarks at their various locations.

Problems being solved

Most corp members are posted to places they are not familiar with, leaving them little fact or knowledge on which to make many of the decisions they'll be making such as choosing preferred PPA, accommodation, place of worship, recreation centers, etc.

How it Works

Corperzwee helps them make better decisions by providing a community where ex-corpers and serving corpers can give reviews of certain locations in a state. With the reviews and ratings provided, new corp members can choose rightly, or prepare their mind and get tips on how to survive in certain places. For instance, experience with a certain landlord can be checked before deciding to rent an apartment :)
